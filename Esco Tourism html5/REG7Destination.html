Queen City of the South- Cebu

Top Things to do

- - Take a Glance of the Past - -

Since Cebu is one of the oldest city and first Spanish settlement in the Philippines, it has a lot of historical attractions to offer to its visitors. The large sea port and air hub has also contributed to modernize the city and the urban culture mix with the old heritage. Learn about the local culture and history with a visit to ancestral houses, museums and preserved landmarks. This will surely give you a unique experience to be remembered.
